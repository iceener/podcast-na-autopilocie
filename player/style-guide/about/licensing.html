In short

  • You can transfer SystemFlow to your co-workers only if you're on TEAM LICENSE and their Webflow logins are within company domain
  • You can transfer SystemFlow to Clients only as an End Product with substantial modifications and this can include Style Guide pages. Your Clients, though, cannot sell, distribute for free or sub-license it
